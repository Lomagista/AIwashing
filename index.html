Conceptual Bias

When we talk about AI, what are we actually talking about? Are we talking about having strong AI that equals or even exceeds human intelligence, or a general, slightly retarded voice assistant like SIRI, or facial recognition, deepfake or even outcome prediction algorithms?

When we talk about machine learning, what are we talking about? When we talk about deep learning, what are we talking about? When we talk about neural networks, what are we talking about?

As unrelated practitioners, there is no shame in being plagued by these issues. Silicon chip-based AI technology has been in development for nearly 70 years and is far from being as new or monolithic as we thought.
